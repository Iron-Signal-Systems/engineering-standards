# Historical Data Integrity

Do not claim tamper-proof history.

Progress through:

1. restricted update and delete authority;
2. controlled corrections and explicit supersession;
3. deterministic record or batch hashes;
4. periodic integrity roots or checkpoints;
5. signing by an independent integrity identity;
6. off-host export;
7. restore-time recomputation and comparison;
8. compromise and re-anchoring procedures.

The defensible claim, after acceptance, is:

> Unauthorized alteration is detectable within the defined integrity-checkpoint
> interval.
